Movie Ranking Project

Rank your favorite movies

stranger things

Stranger Things is a popular science fiction horror series set in the 1980s, revolving around a group of kids in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The show features supernatural elements, government conspiracies, and a nostalgic homage to 80s pop culture.

breaking bad

Breaking Bad is a critically acclaimed crime drama series that follows the transformation of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer. The show explores themes of morality, family, and the consequences of one's choices.

the office

The Office is a mockumentary-style sitcom that depicts the everyday lives of office employees working at Dunder Mifflin, a paper company. The series is known for its unique humor, relatable characters, and memorable quotes.

game of thrones

Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama series based on George R.R. Martin's series of novels. Set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, the show features noble families vying for control of the Iron Throne, political intrigue, and epic battles.

the mandalorian

The Mandalorian is a space Western series set in the Star Wars universe, following the journey of a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy. The show explores themes of honor, loyalty, and the struggle for survival in a lawless land.

the crown

The Crown is a historical drama series that chronicles the reign of Queen Elizabeth II, exploring the personal and political challenges she faces as the leader of the British monarchy. The show delves into significant events in British history and the impact of royal decisions on the nation.
